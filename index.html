CUSTOMIZE YOUR WAY OF SUPPORTING EDUCATION WITH UPCYCLED BOOKS

Who are we?

We are a group of high school entrepreneurs hoping to raise awareness about improper paper waste and support education in developing countries through our bussiness e²

What do we do?

We upcycle old, unreadable books and turn them into organizers such as folders or dividers

Why do we do it?

58 million children in the world do not have access to primary education

10% of our profits will be donated to WorldVision to help decrease this number!
