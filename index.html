Our Cause

Who are we?

We are a group of high school entrepreneurs hoping to raise awareness about improper paper waste and support education in developing countries through our bussiness e²!

What do we do?

We upcycle old, damaged books and turn them into folders, subject dividers and notebooks.

Why do we do it?

58 million children in the world do not have access to primary education

10% of our profits go towards supporting these children by gifting solar lamps and school supplies!

Products

Our products are made from upcycled old books, what gives them really nice look!

We offer replacement for the harmfull plastic organizers, that everyone uses and nobody really realizes how harmfull they are to our nature!
